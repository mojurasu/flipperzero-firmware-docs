◆ RFAL_FDT_LISTEN_A_ADJUSTMENT

#define RFAL_FDT_LISTEN_A_ADJUSTMENT   (276U - 64U)

FDT Listen adjustment for ISO14443A EMVCo 2.6 4.8.1.3 ; Digital 1.1 6.10

276: Time from the rising pulse of the pause of the logic '1' (i.e. the time point to measure the deaftime from), to the actual end of the EOF sequence (the point where the MRT starts). Please note that the ST25R391x uses the ISO14443-2 definition where the EOF consists of logic '0' followed by sequence Y. -64: Further adjustment for receiver to be ready just before first bit
